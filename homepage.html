WE SHAPE YOUR SUCCESS

SANDCAST arose from the need to shorten the development and manufacturing process between the customer and the means of production.

With a vast knowledge in engineering, design, project, tools, casting and metal mechanics, SANDCAST has used the best techniques available on the market, bringing together reliable and quality partners in order to satisfy its customer by reducing manufacturing times and costs.

SOLUTIONS

SANDCAST creates a partnership with its client in order to find the best solution for their needs. It selects the material and manufacturing process that best suits your needs, reducing costs and time...

... develops the project adapting it to the manufacturing process. It designs moulds, core boxes, tools according to the available manufacturing processes.

Tools are manufactured in various processes based on quick response and lower cost.

SANDCAST has a wide range of partners with different processes from the casting of various alloys, finishes, machining, coatings, assembly... providing the customer with a 'ready to use' quality product.
